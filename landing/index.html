What it is

cmail provides organisational mailboxes for a custom domain — inbound delivery, outbound sending, threading, drafts, archive/spam/trash, and an admin console for users, mailboxes, policy, and audit. Designed for organisations that want to own their email infrastructure without operating a server.

It's released under the MIT licence. Use it, fork it, run it for your team, embed it in something larger — that's the point.
